Lo Mein

This delicious Chinese dish is one of my favorites, it's easy to make in just 15 minutes and has all the nutrients you need!

So let's get started on this iconic dish, be sure to follow the instructions carefully!

  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 150 grams of uncooked noodles
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 400 grams of asian vegetable mix
  • 1-2 tablespoons of rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon of hoisin sauce
  1. Boil 1,5 litre of water in a small pan
  2. Add the soy sauce, teaspon of sugar and sesame oil to a small container like a cup
  3. Add the noodles to the boiling water and cook for 5 minutes
  4. Meanwhile fry the veggies on the highest heat that your stove has in a wok pan or skillet
  5. Drain the noodles in a colandar
  6. Keep frying the veggies on the highest heat for 5 minutes total
  7. Take the veggies off the heat and add the mirin
  8. Add the noodles to the wok pan or skillet, add the sauce mixture and hoisin sauce, stir and serve
